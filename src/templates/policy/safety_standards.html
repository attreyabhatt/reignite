Safety Standards

Effective date: January 11, 2026

TryAgainText is committed to protecting users and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We do not allow content that sexualizes minors, promotes or depicts abuse, or facilitates exploitation.

Prohibited Content
  • Any sexual content involving minors
  • Content that promotes, depicts, or facilitates CSAE
  • Requests for sexual content involving minors
  • Grooming, harassment, or exploitation of minors
Reporting & Enforcement
  • Violations result in account suspension or termination
  • We cooperate with law enforcement where required
  • We may retain and provide relevant data to authorities as legally required
How to Report

If you believe content or behavior violates these standards, contact us at support@tryagaintext.com.

User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for complying with applicable laws and these standards. Any attempt to use the service for prohibited content is strictly forbidden.
