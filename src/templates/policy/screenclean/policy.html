ScreenClean Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ScreenClean handles information when you use the app. ScreenClean is a screenshot cleanup app that helps you find and remove screenshots from your device.

1. Information We Access
  • Photos and screenshots on your device: Used only to detect, sort, and help you delete screenshots.
  • Basic app preferences: Stored locally on your device (for example app settings).
  • Analytics data: If enabled, we use Google Analytics to collect usage events such as feature interactions, app version, device model, and approximate region.
  • Advertising data (if ads are enabled): Ads are served through Google AdMob, which may process device identifiers and usage data under its own policies.
2. How We Use Information
  • To scan your screenshots and show cleanup suggestions.
  • To delete screenshots only when you confirm deletion.
  • To improve app stability and user experience.
  • To understand feature usage and improve the app using analytics trends.
  • To display ads when enabled in the app build.
3. Local Processing

ScreenClean is designed to process screenshot analysis on your device. We do not upload your screenshots to our servers for scanning or cleanup operations.

4. Permissions

ScreenClean requests photo/media access so it can find screenshots in your gallery. If you deny this permission, screenshot scanning features will not work.

5. Third-Party Services

ScreenClean may use third-party services such as Google Analytics for usage analytics and Google AdMob for advertising. These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data when required for service delivery (such as ad serving) or if required by law.

7. Data Retention

Any local app data remains on your device unless you remove the app or clear app data. We do not maintain a cloud account or server profile for ScreenClean users.

8. Children's Privacy

ScreenClean is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@tryagaintext.com.
